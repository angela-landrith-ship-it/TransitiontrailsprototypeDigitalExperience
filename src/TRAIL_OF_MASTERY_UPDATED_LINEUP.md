## ğŸ’¡ Why Salesforce Admin First?

### **1. Industry Standard Career Path**
Most Salesforce careers start with the Admin certification. It's the gateway role.

### **2. Foundational Knowledge**
Admins learn the platform fundamentals that Developers and Architects build upon.

### **3. Lower Barrier to Entry**
Intermediate difficulty (vs. Advanced) makes it accessible to career changers and beginners.

### **4. Immediate Job Market Value**
Salesforce Admin is one of the most in-demand entry-level tech roles with strong salary potential.

### **5. Prerequisite for Advanced Roles**
Understanding admin concepts is crucial before diving into development or architecture.
