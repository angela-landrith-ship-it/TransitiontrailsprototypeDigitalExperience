## â“ **FAQs**

### **Q: Do I have to share to LinkedIn?**
**A:** Nope! Sharing is optional but encouraged. It helps your career AND the Transition Trails community.

### **Q: Can I edit Penny's suggested post?**
**A:** Absolutely! Edit as much as you want. Penny gives you a starting point.

### **Q: What if I don't have many LinkedIn connections?**
**A:** Perfect time to start! Your cohort and coaches will engage. Growth starts somewhere.

### **Q: Do points expire?**
**A:** No! Points are cumulative. Once earned, they're yours.

### **Q: Can I see who's ahead of me?**
**A:** Yes! Check the leaderboard to see top performers and your ranking.

### **Q: What do badges do?**
**A:** They show your engagement level on your profile, in community, and on Slack. Plus, they look cool! ðŸ˜Ž

### **Q: Can I share the same achievement twice?**
**A:** You can share it multiple times, but you only earn points once per achievement.

### **Q: How are reactions counted?**
**A:** LinkedIn reactions (likes, celebrates, supports) are aggregated weekly. 5+ reactions = +5 points.
